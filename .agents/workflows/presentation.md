# 🏆 Hackathon Presentation Workflow

This workflow guides you through the high-impact demonstration of Nexus Mobility.

### 1. The Passenger Experience (AI Decision Making)
- Open the Passenger App (website root).
- Ask the AI Chat: *"Should I take the next bus or wait?"*
- Highlight the **AI Comparison Card** showing occupancy.
- Open the Live Map and show the **Smoothly Animated Buses** (GPS interpolation).

### 2. The Driver's Logic (Demand-Responsive)
- Switch to the Driver Console.
- Point to the **👤 Waiting Badges** on the map.
- Trigger an **SOS Signal** (Mechanical/Fire) to show real-time broadcast.

### 3. The Admin Hub (Command & Scalability)
- Switch to the Admin App.
- Observe the **Live SOS Alert** that just arrived.
- Switch the city using the header (e.g., from Mumbai to Wardha).
- Show the **Live Demand Hotspots** and **Vision AI Recovery Logs**.

### 4. Technical Pitch
- Briefly mention the **Google Gemini Pro AI** integration for Chat and Vision.
- Highlight the real-time sync with **Firebase Firestore**.
- Emphasize the **Multi-City Scalability** for Tier 2/3 towns.
